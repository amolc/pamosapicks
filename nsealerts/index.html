What is Automated Algo Stock Trading?

It is the ability to trade stocks using pre-coded trading instructions based on algorithms, executed automatically without human involvement.

How popular is Algorithmic Auto Trading?

60 to 73% of all stock trades are estimated to be executed algorithmically and are primarily used by institutional investors and brokerage houses.

Why should I use Algorithmic Stock Trading?

It ensures the best execution of trades instantly, without emotions, allowing you to trade multiple stocks without staring at a screen, waiting for trades.
